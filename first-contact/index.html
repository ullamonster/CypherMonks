I’ve Discovered a New Realm

Year 1782, 3, 289

I finally managed to narrow down the possibilities and perfect the calculations the portal has been inexplicably spewing at me for four epochs. I have calibrated it properly, only to find that not only is there a new realm, undiscovered as of yet by any of my brothers, but its magnitude supersedes that of our entire world!

It appears they are more advanced in ways we have not seen before, yet primative in the ways of our magic.

This could be a new dawn for us, we may finally see an end to this era.

I must bring this to the others.

brother Goguen
